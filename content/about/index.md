**Yaniv Hatiel** is a computational architect, designer, and educator, and the founder of <a href="https://01architecture.com" target="_blank">**01Architecture**</a>, a London-based design and research studio working at the intersection of architectural design, computation, and performance-driven delivery.

With international practice experience at **Zaha Hadid Architects**, **ODA**, **Kohn Pedersen Fox**, and **PLP Architecture**, Yaniv has contributed to large-scale and complex projects, leading façade design, geometric rationalisation, and BIM-led coordination. His work focuses on translating ambitious design intent into buildable, high-performance architectural systems through advanced computational workflows.

Alongside practice, Yaniv teaches architectural design, parametric design, bioclimatic design, and BIM at university level, and is the founder of <a href="https://archidemy.01architecture.com" target="_blank">**Archidemy**</a>, a digital platform providing hands-on tools, simulations, and automation workflows for architecture students and professionals.
